RMN SHOP

Create a bite-sized, focused shopping experience as a stand-alone app. We worked through many challenges including browse experience, universal shopping cart, and content curation.

After testing, it was clear a single-product purchase flow was disliked.

Content will forever reign supreme

RMN ONBOARDING

Set expectations and show the breadth of our product with a simple onboarding process for the RMN app.

We tried a single screen onboarding with opt-in as well as a more blown out experience.

After all of this, my suggestion for contextual opt-ins won out

FROLIC

Branding and UI work for a activity-making app for friends. Pick an activity, a place, a time, vote, and alert your group.

Users loved seeing real-time results while voting.

Voting systems are addicting & effective

DEVICE CHECK IN/OUT

An internal tool to track who has what testing device checked in or out.

I wanted the ability to place dibs on currently checked out devices.

Internal tools are a great way to flex your design muscles (AKA fun)

PIZZA QUEST

A new way to enjoy pizza and throw shade at your friends who aren't! Weekly pizza challenges, earn points, and invite friends to slay za.

I wanted users to be rewarded by completeing a quest and guilty for bailing.

Adding gamification can be a game-changer for reengagement
